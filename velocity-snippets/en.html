When typing very fast, snippets might accidentally be inserted - namely when a key is still held while pressing the Space key.

Solution: Adjust the gesture delay time in settings individually:

  • Frequent typos when typing fast: Decrease the delay
  • No snippets generated (slow typing): Increase the delay
  • Find your personal optimal balance between speed and precision
